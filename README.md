# Crecy AI

> One Widget. Six AI Business Solutions.

A problem-based AI SaaS for small businesses: the owner picks the problem to solve
(more leads, faster support, quicker quotes, follow-up, paperwork, or sales) and
installs one embeddable widget that runs the right AI agent for the job.

## The plan in one paragraph

MVP = lead capture + customer service + quote assistant, powered by a business
knowledge base, delivered through one async embeddable widget, managed from a mini
CRM dashboard, and protected by usage-based subscriptions. Three more agents
(follow-up, paperwork, sales) ship "light." Built on a 12-table schema with RLS,
public-key + domain-validated widget APIs, usage metering from day one, and a
5–10 business pilot.

See [`ANALYSIS.md`](./ANALYSIS.md) for the full review and the unit-economics math, and
[`PATH_TO_10.md`](./PATH_TO_10.md) for the concrete upgrades that take it to 10/10.
